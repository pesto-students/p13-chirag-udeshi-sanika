Pune, India

Location

Pune is located in the western part of state of Maharashtra in India. It is close to Mumbai, which is the financial capital of India.

About

History

Pune has a big historical significance. It was the seat of the Peshwas from the Maratha Empire. Pune was also home to many freedom fighters like Lokmanya Tilak and Gopal Krishna Gokhale and witnessed several freedom movements.

Known For

Pune is called the 'Vidyecha Maher' which means 'Home of education' because it has large numbers of colleges and institutes across all educational fields. Pune is also an IT hub with a huge population working in the IT sector.

Things to do in Pune

  • Try Maharashtrian food like Marathi Thali, Misal and Sabudana Wada
  • Visit Peshwe Museum, Saras Baug Ganpati, Shaniwar Wada
  • Trekking enthusiasts can go to Sinhagad forts, or numerous smaller hills called tekdis all over Pune.
  • Shop at Laxmi Road and Tulshi Baug for inexpensive yet unique items.
